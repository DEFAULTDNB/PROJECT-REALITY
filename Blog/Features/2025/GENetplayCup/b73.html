Licence to Thrill

Strictly using the lethal Licence to Kill mode paired with pistols, players must compete in heat of 1v1 action with a maximum of 24 players allowed to enter.

The grand finale ussually comes down to the best of five matches, each match being first to 10 points, and the levels are randomly chosen to keep shaking things up for all players.

To enter, you must adhere to the strict rules of using a specific ROM and emulator build. The ROM in question is the 60 Netplay LTK Cup Edition 1.2 by AXDOOMER to be used with Project64 version 3.0.1 emulator, with AQZ Netplay v142.

Previous LTK CUP III & IV winner Graslu00 will also be participating, so free up your diaries, because it will no doubt be incredible to watch these players in action, from February 7th, 2025.
