Worms Armageddon ~ Dec 11, 2024
Worms Armageddon Anniversary

Soil Soldiers

2024 marks the 25th anniversary of the release of Worms Armageddon, the explosive game of turn-based tactical warfare featuring Lumbricus Terrestris, also known as the humble earthworm.

The game, which landed on the Nintendo 64 on December 11th, 1999, featured four-player gaming without the need to pass the pad thanks to the N64's quadruple controller sockets and brought the most frantic, ludicrous combat imaginable to players huddling around their CRT TVs.

Team17 and Digital Eclipse took the opportunity to bring the squidgy invertebrate strategy game to new players digitally on their latest-generation hardware, with releases on PC via Steam, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch on September 26th, 2024.

 

This Anniversary edition will be released on September 26th and will now allow console players a chance to get stuck into the artillery insanity of worms, for the first time. The developers have poured a lot of love into the enhancements, with many quality-of-life improvements and a completely redesigned UI across all platform versions.

Additional Features:

  • Optimized controller support for all consoles.
  • Achievements and Trophies
  • New display features such as modern aspect ratios, custom screen filters, enhanced HD and graphics options.
  • Fully playable Game Boy Color version of Worms Armageddon• An interactive museum that charts the history of the entire series. Featuring never-before-seen interviews with developers of the original and current games.
  • New save system
  • Use over 55 classically weird and wonderful weapons and tools including Earthquake, Freeze and French Sheep Strike.
  • Play single-player Deathmatch plus an exciting 40+ mission campaign
  • Over 30 custom and comedy sound banks (including stiff upper lip, Cyberworms and more).
  • Beloved features from the multi-player mode: Handicapping (add or deduct energy for a team), Allied Teams (team up with others to target another player), ‘Worms Disease’, Home Runs and many, many more!

Worms Armageddon will feature online support, allowing up to six players to team-up or compete. Online is cross-generation so PS4 and PS5 owners will be able to play against each other, as well Xbox One and Xbox Series owners. Worms Armageddon: Anniversary Edition is coming to Nintendo Switch, Xbox One, Xbox Series, PS4 and PS5

The Anniversary Edition: Collectors Edition, which lands physically today (December 11th, 2024), comes with a real-life hard copy of the game (PlayStation or Nintendo Switch), and it also contains a double-sided collectable anniversary coin, Worms stickers, a Weapons & Utilities Guide booklet, a double-sided poster, and three exclusive pins!

Holy Hand Grenade

When Worms Armageddon released in 1999, it scored an average of 82% across a multitude of reviews. The game was widely praised for weapon selection, replayability, sound effects, and graphic style, though it was mainly critiqued for not having all the features of its sibling PC release. Notably, the the Petrol Bomb, Napalm Strike, and Longbow were left out of the N64 version.

The game was re-released recently on Limited Run Games in 2023, with a neat fleshy pink cartridge, though the release was marred by lengthy production times of up to two years for the collector's edition, generally poor reception to the cost-to-value ratio, and the bashed-up condition of the boxes when shipped. The official site also lists Worms Armageddon as two-player, which is odd as it is in fact four-player!

This Anniversary Edition: Collectors Edition hasn't suffered the same treatment; instead it was announced in September of 2024 alongside the digital release.

With an additional six-player online mode, Worms Armageddon now has even more strings to its bow in 2024, so will you be picking this up? Have you been playing it digitally already? What are your thoughts on this game?
