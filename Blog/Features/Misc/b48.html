BattleTanx on Limited Run

Rollin Out

Limited Run Games has announced that they are re-releasing 3DO's post-apocalyptic tank-based blast-a-thons BattleTanx and BattleTanx Global Assault physically for N64 and that they expect to ship preorders around August 2025.

Battle through an urban post-apocalyptic wasteland in these two classic N64 action games!

The games come on limited edition coloured cartridges, but notably the Global Assault cartridge, which previously came in green back in 1999, is now a dusky blue, and the OG BattleTanx is now an odd shade of green.

Previously, Limited Run has released Worms Armageddon on a pink cartridge, Star Wars Shadows of the Empire on a transparent purple cart, and Star Wars Episode One Racer on a half blue, half opaque cartridge.

Limited Run releases are always met with some scepticism because effectively these games are reproduction cartridges with ROMs burnt to them and a modest price tag. Barring the NTSC-U release of Worms Armageddon's value remaining quite high amongst collectors, most games LRG reproduce are still available for sale on second-hand markets as non-repro legitimate items, for far less money than LRG is asking.
