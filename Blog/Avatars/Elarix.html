ReCollect64 Chats No. 24

Elarix ~ Jan 31, 2025
@Elarix

Welcome to ReCollect64 chat Elarix! Let's kick-off this Q&A by describing your presence on social media for people:

I got my start in the Mario 64 hacking scene, but when they discovered "L is real" and other beta content, I wondered if I could find similar stuff for Star Fox 64. Turned out there was a lot of cut content just sitting there, and I thought it was a shame no one cared to look for it yet. Right now I want to unearth and restore as much of it as I can, and hopefully other fans find it as interesting as I do. After that, I want to mod a full sequel to SF64.

When did you get into gaming, any formative recollections?

I was only 3 when my older brother got an N64 for his birthday. As a kid I used to draw Mario and Star Fox levels with markers and build them out of blocks, so I love that I can actually make those a reality now.

What does your retro gaming collection look like?

I'm not really a collector or anything, but I still have a dozen or so N64 cartridges from decades ago, and I still play 'em to this day.

Do you have a favourite console?

It's between the Wii and N64, but given the nostalgia factor, I'd have to go with the N64. I missed the GameCube generation because I was too young to buy my own console.

What games do you play regularly?

Nowadays I play a lot of TF2, Minecraft, and Mario 64 mods on console when I can. Currently playing Rivals II with my younger brother, and we're working our way through the Mario Parties for fun.

What snacks/drinks keep you fuelled up for gaming?

This pizza-flavored Chex mix is DISGUSTING. I can't stop eating it...

Do you have a prized gaming possession?

Just my N64.

Do you have any gaming unicorns left to obtain?

Hey, if anyone has a hi res scan of this old Lylat Map or recognizes the mag, it looks like there's an icon of a lost level: HERE

Are you looking forward to any games in particular, regarding the recent N64 Recomp projects?

That preview of Paper Mario with ray tracing looked amazing! Hopefully the same can be done for Star Fox.

You’re ordering pizza, you get three toppings, what’s your combo?

Sausage, green peppers, and... uh... mushrooms?

What’s your most expensive/thrifty retro purchase to date?

Years ago, while shopping for games for my Wii, I found Paper Mario TTYD in a used Gamestop bin for like $15 bucks. The game looked so weird and different, but I'm glad 11-yo me got it on a whim, or I never would've been introduced to the Mario RPGs.

And Finally: How many consoles do you own?

Just 4: N64, Wii, Switch, and a PlayStation 2 literally just to play Battlefront. If my 3DS counts, 5.

It definitely does! Thank you very much for your time and being part of ReCollect64 chats!
