Brett Jones Has Passed Away

Gone But Never Forgotten ~ July 18, 2024

Influencial and Humble

Sad news today that RARE legend Brett Jones has sadly passed away. Best known for his work on N64's GoldenEye 007 and Perfect Dark, Brett also had an illustrious career in VFX artistry, working on projects including Doctor Who and Star Wars.

On Twitter, various N64 scene members who knew Brett have posted touching tributes, including David Doak, @Graslu00 and @Foslerfer, among others. People have been sharing Brett's fantasic artwork and celebrating his life with nostalgic posts from panels and meet and greets, which really demonstrates the strength and warmth of the N64 community.

An Exhibition to Remember

An exhibition of Brett's work from TV, film, and the gaming industry is planned to be held for two weeks from the 4th of November 2024.

If you would like to donate towards Brett's exhibition, there is a link to his JustGiving crowd funding, which will go towards exhibiting Brett's artwork at the Atkins Gallery in Hinkley.

Here at ReCollect64, we wish to extend our condolences to Brett's family and friends. We will certainly raise a controller in his memory, with a few rounds of GoldenEye and Perfect Dark to celebrate such an influential figure within the gaming industry.
