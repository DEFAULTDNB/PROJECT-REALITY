Super Mario 64 on DC

Dreams Become Reality

Today, hot on the heels of the previous announcement that Doom 64 was being ported to the Dreamcast, Falco Girgis, aka Gyrovorbis, of KallistiOS fame, has announced a port of the beloved plumbers 64-bit adventure, "using the latest GCC 14.1.0 toolchain and the tips of the KallistiOS and GLdc master branches"!

It's a heavy work in progress so far, with texture, clipping, and audio issues, but it's a promising start for the team, who are eager to demonstrate their toolchain and ease-of-use in porting decompiled binaries to the Dreamcast.

Super Mario 64 changed the landscape for 3D platformers and paved the way for analog stick gaming. You can take a look at the progress so far, here:

An Important Port

Theres some work to do yet, because Mario looks incredibly borked, but the basics are there and the performance is phenominal, so it gets me wondering: could we see a Perfect Dark port? Perhaps an Ocarina of Time or Majoras Mask port? Only time will tell!

With Super Mario 64 being ported to DC, the old addage about "Can it run Doom" almost becomes "Can it run Super Mario 64?". As always, I look forward to seeing more N64 games being ported to more consoles in the future!
