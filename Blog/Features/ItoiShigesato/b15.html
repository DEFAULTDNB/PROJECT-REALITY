New Card Found!

Ongoing Quest

The hunt for Operation Cards never ends, and a couple of days ago, thanks to @JosetteTrinkets, we now have a new one added to the ReCollect64 Operation Card repo!

Itoi Shigesato no Bass Tsuri No. 1 Kettei Ban! released in 2000, offering a comfy fishing experience designed by none other than Shigesato Itoi of Earthbound fame.

While originally trawling the net for Operation Cards using Nintendo's own servers, I also discovered this oddly relevant and still functional link for the Ascii Tsuricon fishing controller!

This card brings the total number of titles with an operation card to 49, so I have updated the original chart below to reflect this. You can check out the new scans below!

Are There More?

The answer is, undoubtedly, but are there new individual titles to be found, or just regional variants?

I already know that there are Brazilian, Korean, Australian, and American variants of the standard first-party title's cards, and I also discovered that revised cards exist for million seller/players choice variants too, so there are definitely plenty to catalogue, though sadly, there are very few resources to find them on, and even fewer where decent quality captures have been made.
