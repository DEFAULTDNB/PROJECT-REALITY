UPDATE N64brew Game Jam No. 5

The Winners! ~ Jan 18, 2025

Rewarding ROMs

Back in December, N64brew shared the news that 21 entries had been made for their 5th N64 Game Jam. Today, Buu342 announced the winners of the competition as voted for by the community via their Discord channel:

1st - 112 points - Coin Rush (HailToDodongo) - A stunning side-scroller featuring 2D characters in a 3D world, slugging it out to collect the most coins!

2nd - 102 points - SpaceWaves (Spooky Iluha) - A spacestation defence minigame set across different galaxies.

3rd - 99 Points - Hydra Harmonics (Catch-64) - Taste the music and eat the notes to win!

Worthy Winners

With my personal favourites, Coin Rush and Space Waves, coming in first and second, they really deserved the top spots for offering incredibly well-designed games that are virtually studio standard in terms of features and originality that have been presented in such a charming and playable way.

Hydra Harmonics is also a brilliantly deserving winner of the third spot, with its innovative and musical cooperative lizard-based gameplay!

Congratulations to all who entered; you really showed a plethora of incredible ideas and demonstrated just what is possible on the system in 2025! Thank you to everyone who donated and voted towards this Game Jam. With your help, over $1753 was raised, with the first-place winners, HailToDodongo, donating their winnings to the charities of their choice later this week!

I cannot wait to see if any of these games get updated or continued, and I definitely cannot wait to see what the next Game Jam entails!
